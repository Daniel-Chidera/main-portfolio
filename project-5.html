    Mary's Farm — Animal Farm Website

    HTML5 CSS3 JavaScript

    Overview

    Mary's Farm is an animal farm business based in Port Harcourt, Nigeria, offering a range of farm products including pigs, broilers, fish, maggot farming and eggs. I built their website to help the business reach more buyers and present their products in a clean, professional way online.

    The Problem It Solves

    Farm businesses in Nigeria rarely have websites — most rely on local markets and referrals to find customers. This limits their growth significantly. A website opens Mary's Farm up to a much wider customer base, including bulk buyers, restaurants, and businesses looking for a reliable, consistent supplier in Port Harcourt.

    Key Features

    • Homepage showcasing the farm, its story and its values
    • Products section covering all farm offerings — pigs, broilers, fish, maggot farming and eggs
    • Clear pricing and product details to help buyers make decisions
    • Contact section for direct inquiries and bulk orders
    • Fully responsive design — works on all devices

    How I Built It

    Built with HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I kept the design clean, trustworthy and easy to navigate — the kind of site that makes a buyer feel confident placing an order. I used earthy, natural tones to reflect the farm's identity and connect with the target audience.

    My Role

    This was a client project — I was approached to build the site for Mary's Farm and handled everything from start to finish: design, development and delivery. It was a great opportunity to build something with real business impact for a local entrepreneur in Port Harcourt.
