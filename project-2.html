    NTDS — NextGen Technova Digital Solutions

    HTML5 CSS3 JavaScript

    Overview

    NTDS is a Lagos-based tech startup offering web development, mobile app development, and digital marketing services. I designed and built their full company website from scratch as a co-founder of the business — giving the startup a professional online presence to attract clients and showcase its offerings.

    The Problem It Solves

    As a new tech startup in Lagos, NTDS needed a credible, professional website to establish trust with potential clients. Without a strong online presence, it's nearly impossible to compete in the digital services market. The website bridges that gap — presenting the company's services clearly and making it easy for clients to reach out.

    Key Features

    • Full multi-page website: Home, Services, About, Blog, and Contact
    • Services section showcasing Web Development, Mobile App Development, and Digital Marketing
    • Step-by-step process/workflow section explaining how the company works with clients
    • Technology stack showcase with brand icons
    • FAQ accordion for common client questions
    • Staff login portal for internal team use
    • Fully responsive — works on mobile, tablet, and desktop
    • Contact form for client inquiries

    How I Built It

    The entire site was built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no frameworks — which kept it lightweight and fast. I focused on clean layout, readable typography, and smooth interactions. The responsive design was handled with CSS media queries to ensure the site looks great across all screen sizes. The site is hosted on GitHub Pages for fast, reliable, free deployment.

    My Role

    I was the sole developer on this project, responsible for everything from design to deployment. This was a real-world project built for an actual business, not a personal exercise — which meant client expectations, brand consistency, and professional quality were all priorities from day one.
