    • AUTOREFUEL: A SMART PETROL ORDERING SYSTEM

    Autorefuel is an online platform designed to simplify the fuel ordering process. It allows users to easily select their fuel type, customize the quantity, and securely place an order for delivery. With a focus on convenience and user experience, Autorefuel ensures a smooth, responsive, and hassle-free process for customers looking to refuel their vehicles without leaving home.

    PROJECT OVERVIEW

    Purpose:

    The Autorefuel website was developed to provide users with an easy, convenient platform for ordering fuel online. The goal was to create a seamless, user-friendly experience that allows customers to select their preferred fuel type, quantity, and delivery options, and complete the transaction securely.

    FEATURES

    • Fuel Selection & Customization: Users can choose fuel types and customize the quantity (in liters) to meet their needs.
    • Order Tracking: Customers can track their orders and delivery status through their user account.
    • Secure Payment System: The website offers secure, integrated payment options to ensure smooth and safe transactions.
    • Responsive Design: The website is fully optimized for mobile and desktop, ensuring an excellent user experience across all devices.
