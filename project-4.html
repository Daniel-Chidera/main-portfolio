    Treehouse Afriq — Restaurant Website

    HTML5 CSS3 JavaScript

    Overview

    Treehouse Afriq is a restaurant based in Port Harcourt, Nigeria. I built their full website to give the brand a professional online presence and make it easy for customers to explore their menu, learn about the restaurant, and get in touch directly.

    The Problem It Solves

    Without a website, restaurants in Port Harcourt rely solely on social media and word of mouth — which limits their reach and makes it hard for new customers to find reliable information. A dedicated website gives Treehouse Afriq a credible, permanent home on the internet where customers can always find them.

    Key Features

    • Restaurant homepage with strong brand identity and visuals
    • Menu showcase displaying the restaurant's offerings
    • About section telling the story of the brand
    • Contact section with location and inquiry options
    • Fully responsive design optimised for mobile users

    How I Built It

    The site was built with HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I focused on warm visuals, clean layout and smooth navigation to reflect the restaurant's brand and create an inviting experience for visitors. The design was built mobile-first since most customers in Port Harcourt browse on their phones.

    My Role

    I was the sole developer on this project — handling everything from design and development to final delivery for the client. This was a real client project built for an actual business in Port Harcourt.
